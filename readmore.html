Aspirin as a pain reliever

Aspirin is known as an acetylsalicylic acid. It also belongs to a group of medicines called
Non-Steroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs). Aspirin is an everyday painkiller for aches and pains such as;
headache, toothache, and period pain. It can also be used to treat
colds, and flu-like symptoms, and to bring down a high temperature.
